ABOUT SCHOOL OF SCIENCE

School of Science was started in 2007 with a view to provide undergraduate and post graduate students an opportunity to be a part of the unique learning methodology of the university, which lays emphasis on academic excellence combined with industry oriented training. With the boom in information technology and more and more sophistication in instrumentation techniques, there is now a very thin dividing line between the various disciplines of science. Therefore, there is a greater need for flexibility in scientific thought as well as training manpower on an interdisciplinary plane. With this thought in view, the SVKM's NMIMS introduced, highly innovative and unique interdisciplinary courses at the School of Science from the academic year 2007-2008.

The goal of the School of Science is to be a Center of Excellence in the domain of Pure and Applied Science by providing quality education and research.

History

The NMIMS Deemed to be University, which began by offering programs in Management, enjoying international recognition as one of the leading institutions of Management training, has now established a School of Technology, a School of Pharmacy, a School of Commerce and a School of Science with a view to provide undergraduate and postgraduate students an opportunity to be a part of the unique learning methodology of the university, which lays emphasis on academic excellence combined with industry oriented training.

The C. B. Patel Research Centre for Chemistry and Biological Sciences, a prime institution borne out of the foresight of the SVKM and its constant endeavour towards excellence in Science and Technology, has during the last 17 years not only established itself as a leading Research Centre but has also provided training to graduate students for manning posts in the Pharmaceutical industry, in para medical fields and more recently in clinical research organisations.

M.SC.- BIOLOGICAL SCIENCES

The NMIMS (Deemed to be University) is introducing the M.Sc. programme in Biological Sciences from August 2012. The salient feature of this programme is the emphasis being laid on the overall development of laboratory skills of a student coupled with an expansion of his/her knowledge base through an interdisciplinary course work comprising lectures and practicals in the first year of the course and subsequently fine tuning these skills in a particular area of specialization.

Duration
  • The M.Sc. is a two years course which includes four trimesters and a six month project work.
Intake
  • A total of 10 seats are available for the M.Sc. program in Biological Sciences
Eligibility
  • For the M. Sc. program in Biological Sciences, a candidate must have passed the three year B.Sc. examination of any recognized University and scored not less than 50% in any one of the following subjects - Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Botany, Zoology and Life Sciences
Programme Objectives
  • Modern facilities to provide ambience and support for curricular and extra-curricular activities for the overall development of students.
  • Dedicated, qualified faculty to ensure high standard of teaching, learning and evaluation processes.
  • Periodic review and revision of curricula based on feedback from the industry with quick response to ensure the relevance of the programmes to the changing needs of industry.
  • Trimester system with proper planning to utilize the resources effectively and efficiently.
  • Industry based project work as part of the curricula to get recognition and reward to the students in the form of job offer or support for further studies and research.
  • The "Industry Interface Program" has been initiated to keep the students abreast of the latest trends in the industry/research organizations through industrial visits and guest lectures.
  • The program is a full time program which is offered from Monday to Saturday.

M.SC.- STATISTICS

Statistics is a rapidly, expanding discipline with openings in practically all industries and service sectors. This is particularly true for the fast growing Insurance sector, which heavily depends on statisticians and actuaries. Unfortunately, the limited availability of seats for the Masters Programme at the Universities level has curtailed academic growth and upward mobility of graduates in Statistics. Moreover, the programmes currently being offered by most Universities lack flexibility and ability to respond to the immediate demands of market forces. This, in general often results in students being qualified but they are not employable.

Duration
  • The rigorous course work is spread over 6 trimesters, including a Research Project.
Intake
  • A total of 20 seats are available for the M.Sc. program in Statistics.
Eligibility
  • A student seeking admission to the M.Sc. Statistics program must have passed the B.Sc. Examination in Statistics of any recognized University and scored not less than 60% marks.
Programme Objectives
  • Modern facilities to provide ambience and support for curricular and extra-curricular activities for the overall development of students.

  • Periodic review and revision of curricula based on feedback from the industry with quick response to ensure the relevance of the programmes to the changing needs of industry.

  • Industry based project work as part of the curricula to get recognition and reward to the students in the form of job offer or support for further studies and research.
  • The "Industry Interface Program" has been initiated to keep the students abreast of the latest trends in the industry/research organizations through industrial visits and guest lectures.
  • The program is a full time program which is offered from Monday to Saturday.
  • Every student will have to complete a research project, preferably industry oriented

MASTER OF PHYSIOTHERAPY

The NMIMS (Deemed to be University) has introduced Master of Physiotherapy program in July 2012. The salient feature of these programmes is the emphasis being laid on gaining advance understanding, knowledge and exclusive skills in the specialized area. The course will enable students with in-depth clinical reasoning, update them with recent advances in professional practice and enhance their ability to carry out a comprehensive, logical, safe, subjective and physical examination.

Duration
  • The course is a 2 years full time program.
Intake
  • A total of 20 seats are available for the Master of Physiotherapy Program.
Eligibility

For the Master in Physiotherapy program, a candidate must have

  • Passed the full time Bachelor's Degree (10 + 2 + 4 years + 6 months of internship) in Physiotherapy from institutions recognized by the Indian Association of Physiotherapy (IAP) with minimum 50% marks.
  • Done Internship program of 6 months from a college or University recognized by IAP
  • Membership of IAP.
Programme Objectives
  • Modern facilities to provide ambience and support for curricular and extra-curricular activities for the overall development of students.
  • Dedicated, qualified faculty to ensure high standard of teaching, learning and evaluation processes.
  • Periodic review and revision of curricula based on feedback from the industry with quick response to ensure the relevance of the programmes to the changing needs of industry.
  • Trimester system with proper planning to utilize the resources effectively and efficiently.
