About Us


Sailors and their legendary voyages have long fascinated storytellers and audiences alike. Marine engineers are modern sailors. And their jobs are just as exciting. They are responsible for the safe running of all engines, machinery and their maintenance, repair and construction. They also supervise engine crew involved in operating the machines.
