WEBSITE DESIGN

CLEAN AND MINIMAL

We design and build websites that fit the screens people use.

Custom Design We believe that every client is distinct, every project unique. Our style is clean, uncluttered, and steeped in the principals of design. What we lack in über-creativity is made up for in precision hierarchy.

Responsive Design Responsive Design is an art in understanding expectations at a variety of screens sizes. We were early adopters of the technique, continue to push its bounds, and include it in every project we do.

CONTENT STRATEGY

The first step towards an authentic,
comprehensive website that does what’s its supposed to do.

Research We need to understand your business, your customers, and your goals before we can solve your problems. Research is the cornerstone to creating a site that will perform to the highest level of expectation.

Wireframes are the bible from which we swear our oath. A guiding light for design, development, and content. Cumbersome and oft overwrought, they are simultaneously sledgehammer and scalpel.

Development

Development based on rock-solid open source platforms.

Standards We believe in coding up a site the right way. That means Standards. Yes, standards change which is why we strive to stay up on the latest. HTML5, CSS3, jQuery; Got it.

Front End While we mostly build on WordPress, it’s not exclusive. We also develop for other platforms such as Ghost and Magento. We also do straight HTML5, CSS3, Git, and Sass.

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript We build on HTML5. It’s open source, rock solid, and easy for you to use. Our core template is built on Hybrid Core, the most forward thinking framework on the planet.
