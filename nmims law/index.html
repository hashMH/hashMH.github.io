About School

NMIMS University, one of the top universities in the country, offers Law programs under NMIMS School of Law. Being a leader in providing quality education, NMIMS offers 2 unique programs under the School of Law, which is B.A., LL.B. (Hons.) and B.B.A., LL.B. (Hons.) ( Integrated Dual Degree Program)

Program Features

  • First "School of Law" in State Maharashtra to have Bar Council of India's approval for 5 years integrated Double Degree Honors Pogram, that is B.A., LL.B. (Hons.) and B.B.A., LL.B. (Hons.), since 3013-14.
  • First "School of Law" (SOL) in India that shall provide (Hons.) Specialization in Corporate Laws, Intellectual Property Laws and Taxation Laws.
  • Trimester based terms.
  • Curriculum for building legal skills.
  • Qualified, experienced sincere and dedicated faculties from academia and industry.
  • Guest lectures by Leading Luminaries from Law firms, Judiciary, Lawyers, Industry etc.

Vision

To constantly revisit and redefine the benchmark set in the field of "Legal" educational excellence in India and abroad. To better the 'Best', so as to be leader in the field of learning & teaching "Law" for its practical application in ever changing circumstances.

Mission

To be competent and successful in ever changing and challenging circumstances at national and international level, in the field of "Law" for justice to individual, society & Corporate World as well.
