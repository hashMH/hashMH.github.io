SPP SPTM LINEAGE

Shobhaben Pratapbhai Patel School of Pharmacy & Technology Management (SPPSPTM) was founded in 2006 with a vision to serve the healthcare system of our country. The courses are tailor-made to fulfill the requirements of the growing Pharma Industry & are reviewed regularly in consultation with our Board of Studies, which comprises people from Pharma Industry, Research, FDA and top academicians from all over India.

Vision

To develop a model Pharmacy School at International level inculcating research trends in Pharmaceutical Science and Technology Management.

Mission

To produce self-motivated and self reliant young pharmacists catering to the needs of Industry,Academia and Research.To create a centre of excellence for R&D in the field of pharmaceutical sciences with inter disciplinary approach in emerging area of Science and Technology with focus on Industry-Academia interaction and cooperation at National and International level.To interlink Pharmaceutical Sciences with Biological / life Sciences and Management Education.To provide Life Long Learning opportunities in Pharm. Sciences.

M.Pharm-Two Year Program

M. PHARM (PHARMACEUTICS)
  • The course addresses a scientific area, which at present, is the basis for some of the main methods of research in the area of development of pharmaceutical products. It is designed to familiarize the student with the fundamental principles pertaining to and the techniques employed in the formulation design for pharmaceutical agents. Introduction of the student to research techniques in the pharmaceutical sciences is made at the initial stage of their studies, which enriches their understanding and application of their knowledge during their research projects. Student will complete the course with advanced knowledge and skills applicable for a variety of practice settings in dosage form design, manufacturing technology, regulatory aspects, patents, quality control and quality assurance.
M. PHARM (PHARMACEUTICAL CHEMISTRY)
  • SPPSPTM offers two years M. Pharm with Pharmaceutical Chemistry as specialization to pharmacy graduates who wish to take up pharma-chemistry as their profession.
  • In the first year of this program, students learn 8 technical subjects and one subject of English and communication. The subjects cover the areas of medicinal chemistry, drug design, molecular modeling, synthetic chemistry, instrumentation and international regulation for registration of API in Europe and USA.
  • In the second year students take up two projects, one major in the cases of specialization spreading over two trimesters and second minor in the cases other than specialization (one of the elective) spreading over one trimester.
M. PHARM (PHARMACOLOGY)
  • The course is of two years duration. The first year curriculum is designed with exposure to advances in pharmacology, wherein the candidate has potential to equip himself/herself with all preclinical pharmacological, toxicological and safety pharmacological studies. Furthermore, he/she gets adequate exposure of clinical research methods, newer molecular biology techniques.
  • The second year is mainly through dissertation on the topic of industrial research and development, new drug discovery etc. Thus the two year program will enable pharmacology student to cope up with present day need of preclinical and clinical research in new drug discovery and development.
M. PHARM (QUALITY ASSURANCE)
  • The course curriculum has been designed to familiarise students with good manufacturing practices, ICH guidelines, calibration, validation, documentation and quality audits, with a view to create total quality consciousness. The course offers wide exposure to research fields, such as formulation development including NDDS and analytical method development, which includes validation, impurity profiling, stability indicating assay methods, preparation of documents like standard testing procedures and standard operating procedures.
  • The wide knowledge earned in Quality Assurance will allow the student to fit into the pharmaceutical development area.
M.PHARM IN DRUG DEVELOPMENT & REGULATORY SCIENCES (DDRS)
  • SPPSPTM, SVKM'S NMIMS, Mumbai offers INNOVATIVE, JOB ORIENTED M. Pharm. Course in Drug Development and Regulatory Sciences supported by Industries from Session 2013-14 which has ready Employability in Drugs and Pharmaceutical Industry and Regulatory Agencies in India and Overseas.
  • M. Pharm.(DDRS) is of Two years Duration after B. Pharm.
  • The areas covered are-Drug Development and Regulations in India and International Requirements (US, UK, EU, WHO, ICH, PIC etc., Drug Development Process and Quality Assurance, Business Opportunities and Regulations, Regulatory Overviews of Biologics , Emerging Business Opportunities (Medical Devices) and Regulatory Overviews, Regulatory Inspections including Environmental Controls, Designs and Validation, Quality System-QbD, Design and Control Space and Documentations.

M. Pharm + MBA (Pharma Tech & Healthcare Management) - Three Year Program

M.PHARM. (PHARMACEUTICS) + MBA (PHARMA TECH AND HEALTH CARE MANAGEMENT)
  • This is a 3 year integrated program spread over 9 trimesters, leading to Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm. - Pharmaceutics) and Masters in Business Administration MBA (Pharma Tech & Healthcare Management). The candidate will acquire postgraduate dual degrees, one in technical field (research) and second in managerial skills in Pharma sector. This also saves one academic year. This program is conducted by well-experienced senior faculty in addition to visiting professors/ industry experts.
  • In the first year of this program, students learn 8 technical subjects and 12 management subjects. The technical subjects cover the areas of Drug Regulatory Affairs and Quality Assurances, Advances in Pharmaceutical Technology, Dosage Form Technology, Biopharmaceutics and Pharmacokinetics, Industrial Pharmacy and Packaging. The management subjects cover the areas of Business Communication, Management of Technology & Innovation, Business Economics, Principles and Practice of Management, Marketing Management, Operations Research, Organizational Behaviour, Pharmaceutical Marketing, Statistical Methods, Project Management and Cost Accounting.
  • In the first two trimesters of second year, students take up a major project on technical subject. Third trimester of second year covers 8 subjects. First two trimesters of third year covers of 10 subjects, along with electives as mentioned in the curriculum. In the third trimester of the third year, students take up management internship program.

Pharm.- Quality Assurance) and Masters in Business Administration MBA (Pharma Tech & Healthcare Management).
